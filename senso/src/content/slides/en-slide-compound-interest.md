# Compound interest

**The most powerful force in personal finance**

---

## How it works

Compound interest means you earn interest **on your interest**.

> €1,000 at 7%/year:
> Year 1: €1,070
> Year 10: €1,967
> Year 30: €7,612

The longer the time horizon, the more dramatic the effect.

---

## The rule of 72

Divide 72 by your annual return to find how many years it takes to double your money.

| Return | Years to double |
|---|---|
| 3% | 24 years |
| 6% | 12 years |
| 10% | 7.2 years |

---

## The other side: debt

Compound interest works **against you** when you borrow.

Credit card at 20% APR: a €1,000 balance unpaid for 3 years becomes **€1,728**.

> Pay off high-interest debt first. Always.

---

## Why starting early is everything

Two people invest €200/month at 7%/year:

| | Starts at 25 | Starts at 35 |
|---|---|---|
| Total invested | €96,000 | €72,000 |
| Value at 65 | **€525,000** | **€243,000** |

> 10 years earlier → more than double the outcome.
